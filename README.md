# POWER-BI-
E-Commerce Sales Analysis Report

Visuals - 
Sales by Country, 
Sales by Ship Mode, 
Sales by Market, 
Sales by Category, 
Sales by State

KPIs (Card Visuals) -
Sum of Sales, 
Sum of Profit, 
Sum of Quantity, 
Sum of Shipping Cost

Slicers -
Market (Countries),
Category (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology)

Usage - 
Download and open in Power BI.
Use slicers to filter data and explore visuals.
